## 👋 OpenWearLab

OpenWearLab is a small playground for experimental, open-source wearable devices — blending sensors, feedback, and a touch of curiosity into new forms of interaction.🧠✨  

From biofeedback-driven ideas to playful connected toys 💡🔌  
Everything here is open, hackable, and meant to be extended — whether you're curious, creative, or just a little bit adventurous 😉
